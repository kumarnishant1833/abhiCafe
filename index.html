About Us

Serving Bharthal with Honest & Fast Digital Services

Abhi Cafe Point is a one-stop digital service centre located in the heart of Bharthal Village, Delhi. We are conveniently situated near Govt. Sarvodaya Bal Vidyalaya School on Bijwasan Road, making us easily accessible to residents, students, and local businesses.

We understand the digital needs of our community — whether it's a school project print-out, getting a PAN card made, repairing your laptop, or paying an online bill. Our experienced team handles it all with speed, care, and transparency.
